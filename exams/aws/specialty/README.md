# AWS Specialty Certifications

Specialty certifications validate deep expertise in specific AWS domains. AWS has been consolidating specialty certs into role-based associate/professional certifications.

## Available Certifications

### Active

| Certification | Exam Code | Duration | Cost | Focus |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| [Security](security-scs-c02/) | SCS-C02 | 170 min | $300 | AWS security services, incident response, encryption |
| [Advanced Networking](advanced-networking-ans-c01/) | ANS-C01 | 170 min | $300 | Complex networking, hybrid connectivity, DNS |

### Retiring Soon

| Certification | Exam Code | Retirement Date | Replacement |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Machine Learning](machine-learning-mls-c01/) | MLS-C01 | March 31, 2026 | ML Engineer Associate (MLA-C01) |

### Retired

| Certification | Exam Code | Retired | Replacement |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Data Analytics](data-analytics-das-c01/) | DAS-C01 | April 2024 | Data Engineer Associate (DEA-C01) |
| [Database](database-dbs-c01/) | DBS-C01 | April 2024 | Data Engineer Associate (DEA-C01) |
| [SAP on AWS](sap-on-aws-pas-c01/) | PAS-C01 | April 2024 | None (niche specialty) |

## Recommended Experience

- 2+ years of hands-on experience in the specialty domain
- Associate-level certification or equivalent knowledge
- Deep expertise in the specific technology area

## Study Tips

- Focus heavily on the specific domain — these exams go deep, not broad
- Hands-on experience in the specialty area is critical
- Security (SCS-C02) is the most widely applicable specialty cert
- Check retirement dates before starting preparation
