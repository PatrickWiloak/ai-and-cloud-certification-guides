# FinOps Certified Practitioner - Study Plan

## 6-Week Comprehensive Study Schedule

### Week 1: FinOps Framework Foundations

#### Day 1-2: FinOps Principles and Lifecycle
- [ ] Read the FinOps Framework overview on finops.org
- [ ] Study the six FinOps principles in detail
- [ ] Understand the three lifecycle phases (Inform, Optimize, Operate)
- [ ] Review the FinOps maturity model (Crawl, Walk, Run)
- [ ] Take notes on key terms and definitions
- [ ] Review Notes: `notes/01-finops-framework.md`

#### Day 3-4: FinOps Personas and Organization
- [ ] Study all FinOps personas (executive, engineering, finance, procurement)
- [ ] Understand the FinOps team structure and responsibilities
- [ ] Learn how FinOps integrates with existing organizational structures
- [ ] Review case studies of FinOps adoption
- [ ] Review Notes: `notes/05-organizational-alignment.md`

#### Day 5-6: Cloud Billing Fundamentals
- [ ] Study AWS billing concepts (Cost Explorer, CUR, Organizations)
- [ ] Study Azure billing concepts (Cost Management, Management Groups)
- [ ] Study GCP billing concepts (Billing Reports, BigQuery export)
- [ ] Understand billing hierarchy across providers
- [ ] Review Notes: `notes/02-cost-visibility.md`

#### Day 7: Week 1 Review
- [ ] Review all notes from the week
- [ ] Create flashcards for key terms
- [ ] Take a mini quiz on FinOps principles and lifecycle
- [ ] Identify areas needing more study

### Week 2: Inform Phase Deep Dive

#### Day 8-9: Cost Allocation and Tagging
- [ ] Study tagging strategies and best practices
- [ ] Learn mandatory vs optional tag categories
- [ ] Understand tag governance and enforcement
- [ ] Study cost allocation methods (direct, shared, amortized)
- [ ] Review Notes: `notes/02-cost-visibility.md`

#### Day 10-11: Showback and Chargeback
- [ ] Understand showback models and their advantages
- [ ] Understand chargeback models and their challenges
- [ ] Learn how to handle shared costs
- [ ] Study unallocated and unallocatable costs
- [ ] Practice creating cost allocation scenarios

#### Day 12-13: Budgeting, Forecasting, and Unit Economics
- [ ] Study budgeting techniques for cloud spending
- [ ] Learn forecasting methods and tools
- [ ] Understand unit economics concepts
- [ ] Study key metrics (cost per customer, cost per transaction)
- [ ] Practice interpreting cost reports and dashboards

#### Day 14: Week 2 Review
- [ ] Review all Inform phase concepts
- [ ] Practice with Inform phase scenarios
- [ ] Take a mini quiz on cost allocation and visibility
- [ ] Update flashcards with new terms

### Week 3: Optimize Phase - Rate Optimization

#### Day 15-16: Reserved Instances and Savings Plans
- [ ] Study Reserved Instance types (Standard, Convertible)
- [ ] Learn Savings Plan options (Compute, EC2, SageMaker)
- [ ] Understand Committed Use Discounts (GCP)
- [ ] Compare payment options (All Upfront, Partial, No Upfront)
- [ ] Review Notes: `notes/03-rate-optimization.md`

#### Day 17-18: Spot and Preemptible Instances
- [ ] Study spot instance pricing and behavior
- [ ] Learn when to use spot vs on-demand vs reserved
- [ ] Understand interruption handling strategies
- [ ] Review use cases (batch, CI/CD, testing)

#### Day 19-20: Volume and Enterprise Discounts
- [ ] Study Enterprise Discount Programs (EDPs)
- [ ] Learn about volume-based discounts
- [ ] Understand private pricing agreements
- [ ] Review marketplace purchasing options

#### Day 21: Week 3 Review
- [ ] Review all rate optimization concepts
- [ ] Practice calculating savings from different discount types
- [ ] Take a mini quiz on rate optimization
- [ ] Update study notes

### Week 4: Optimize Phase - Usage Optimization

#### Day 22-23: Right-sizing
- [ ] Study right-sizing methodology
- [ ] Learn how to analyze CPU, memory, and network utilization
- [ ] Understand provider recommendation tools
- [ ] Review before/after right-sizing case studies
- [ ] Review Notes: `notes/04-usage-optimization.md`

#### Day 24-25: Waste Reduction
- [ ] Identify common types of cloud waste
- [ ] Study idle resource detection methods
- [ ] Learn about scheduling and automation for dev/test environments
- [ ] Understand storage waste (orphaned volumes, snapshots)

#### Day 26-27: Architectural Optimization
- [ ] Study serverless optimization patterns
- [ ] Learn about containerization for cost efficiency
- [ ] Understand auto-scaling for cost management
- [ ] Review storage tiering strategies

#### Day 28: Week 4 Review
- [ ] Review all usage optimization concepts
- [ ] Practice with optimization scenarios
- [ ] Take a mini quiz on waste reduction and right-sizing
- [ ] Review fact sheet and fill knowledge gaps

### Week 5: Operate Phase and Governance

#### Day 29-30: Governance and Policies
- [ ] Study FinOps governance frameworks
- [ ] Learn policy creation and enforcement
- [ ] Understand guardrails and compliance requirements
- [ ] Review automation for policy enforcement
- [ ] Review Notes: `notes/05-organizational-alignment.md`

#### Day 31-32: FinOps Tools and Automation
- [ ] Study native cloud cost tools (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- [ ] Review third-party FinOps tools (Apptio, CloudHealth)
- [ ] Understand automation capabilities
- [ ] Learn about FinOps APIs and integrations

#### Day 33-34: Organizational Change and Culture
- [ ] Study change management for FinOps adoption
- [ ] Learn techniques for building cost awareness
- [ ] Understand executive sponsorship and buy-in
- [ ] Review communication strategies for FinOps

#### Day 35: Week 5 Review
- [ ] Review all Operate phase concepts
- [ ] Practice with governance and policy scenarios
- [ ] Take a comprehensive practice exam
- [ ] Identify remaining weak areas

### Week 6: Final Preparation

#### Day 36-37: Practice Exams
- [ ] Take full-length practice exam 1
- [ ] Review all incorrect answers thoroughly

- [ ] Note patterns in missed questions

#### Day 38-39: Weak Area Review
- [ ] Review topics missed on practice exams
