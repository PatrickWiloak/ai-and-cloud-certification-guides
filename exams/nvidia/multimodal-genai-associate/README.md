# NVIDIA Certified Associate - Multimodal Generative AI (NCA-MMGA)

## Exam Overview

The NVIDIA Certified Associate - Multimodal Generative AI certification validates foundational knowledge of multimodal AI systems that process and generate content across text, images, audio, and video modalities using NVIDIA tools and platforms.

**Exam Code:** NCA-MMGA
**Exam Duration:** 60 minutes
**Number of Questions:** 50-60 questions
**Exam Format:** Multiple choice
**Cost:** $135 USD
**Validity:** 2 years
**Prerequisites:** Basic understanding of AI/ML concepts

## Exam Domains

### Domain 1: Multimodal AI Fundamentals (25%)
- Types of modalities (text, image, audio, video)
- Multimodal model architectures
- Vision-language models (VLMs)
- Cross-modal alignment and fusion
- Use cases for multimodal AI

### Domain 2: Image and Video Generation (25%)
- Diffusion models and image generation
- Text-to-image and image-to-image
- Video generation fundamentals
- NVIDIA Picasso and visual content creation
- Image editing and inpainting

### Domain 3: Text and Speech Modalities (20%)
- LLMs for multimodal understanding
- Text-to-speech and speech-to-text
- NVIDIA Riva for speech AI
- Audio processing fundamentals
- Multimodal prompting

### Domain 4: NVIDIA Multimodal Tools (15%)
- NVIDIA NIM for multimodal models
- NVIDIA NeMo for multimodal training
- Build.nvidia.com for model exploration
- NVIDIA AI Enterprise for deployment
- Multimodal model APIs

### Domain 5: Applications and Best Practices (15%)
- Multimodal RAG (retrieval augmented generation)
- Content moderation for multimodal content
- Evaluation of multimodal systems
- Ethical considerations
- Real-world applications
