# NVIDIA Certified Professional - Networking (NCP-NET)

## Exam Overview

The NVIDIA Certified Professional - Networking certification validates expertise in designing, deploying, and managing NVIDIA networking solutions including InfiniBand, Spectrum Ethernet, RDMA technologies, and network management with UFM for high-performance computing and AI data center environments.

**Exam Code:** NCP-NET
**Exam Duration:** 120 minutes
**Number of Questions:** 60-70 questions
**Exam Format:** Multiple choice
**Cost:** $200 USD
**Validity:** 2 years
**Prerequisites:** Recommended experience with data center networking and HPC environments

## Exam Domains

### Domain 1: InfiniBand Technology (25%)
- InfiniBand architecture and protocol layers
- InfiniBand speeds (HDR, NDR, XDR)
- Subnet management and routing
- Partitioning and quality of service
- InfiniBand diagnostics and troubleshooting

### Domain 2: Spectrum Ethernet (20%)
- NVIDIA Spectrum switch family
- Cumulus Linux and NVIDIA DOCA
- Ethernet switching features for AI workloads
- RoCE (RDMA over Converged Ethernet) configuration
- Network automation and programmability

### Domain 3: RDMA and RoCE (20%)
- RDMA fundamentals and benefits
- RoCE v1 and RoCE v2 protocols
- PFC (Priority Flow Control) and ECN configuration
- GPUDirect RDMA for GPU networking
- Performance tuning for RDMA workloads

### Domain 4: Network Topologies for AI (15%)
- Fat-tree topology design
- Rail-optimized networks
- Dragonfly and other topologies
- Adaptive routing
- Network sizing for AI clusters

### Domain 5: UFM and Network Management (20%)
- NVIDIA UFM (Unified Fabric Manager)
- Network health monitoring and diagnostics
- Telemetry and analytics
- Fabric management and provisioning
- Troubleshooting with UFM tools
